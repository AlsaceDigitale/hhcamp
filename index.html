HACKATHON

Hacking Health Camp is an event dedicated to breaking the barriers of innovation in healthcare by creating a collaboration space between health professionnals, designers, hackers, makers and entrepreneur.
During the first edition, health professionals and hackers express their expectations : about topics such as ethic, health datas, interoperability, what is the worldwide evolution of digital in healthcare ? Where are the technical limits ? What are legal issues and solutions ?
So this year, Hacking Health & Alsace Digitale provide a global event for all people interesting in health innovation, inspiring both health professionals and hackers about their common amazing possibilities in tomorrow’s health.

EVENTS

Health Data Day

1 day of workshops and conferences about producing and consuming health data around 3 aspects : technical, medical, privacy & law
When ? thursday
For who ? health professionnals, developers, health industries employees and entrepreneurs

Health Pitch Challenge

1 evening party dedicated to health startups with investors present. 5 startups wil pitch, attendees will vote with fake banknote. Winner will be the one who raie most money
When ? thursday evening
For who ? health investors, people interested in health, entrepreneurs

Future of Health

1 day insight conferences on health future by international health mentors
When ? friday
For who ? health professionnals, developers, health industries employees, entrepreneurs

Hackathon

48h marathon of development with health professionnals, developers and designers on challenges submid on our sparkboard. Your goal is to create a functional prototype. On sunday a jury will give honorable mentions and prizes to best prototypes.
When ? start friday evening, end sunday afternoon
For who ? health professionnals, developers, designers, entrepreneurs
